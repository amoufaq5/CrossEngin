## Context

## Decision

## Consequences

- **Positive:** what we gain.
- **Negative:** what we give up or take on as cost.
- **Neutral:** what changes but isn't strictly better or worse.
- **Reversibility:** how hard is it to undo this decision later? What would it cost in time, money, or risk?
